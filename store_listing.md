## Full Description
Stay organized and get things done with ToDo, the beautifully designed task management app that makes productivity effortless.

Whether you are managing daily errands, work projects, or personal goals, ToDo provides everything you need to stay on top of your tasks without the clutter of overly complex tools.

**Key Features:**

- **Smart Task Organization** - Create, edit, and categorize tasks with intuitive controls
- **Priority Levels** - Mark tasks as high, medium, or low priority to focus on what matters most
- **Due Date Tracking** - Set deadlines and never miss an important date again
- **Slide Actions** - Swipe to complete, edit, or delete tasks with satisfying gestures
- **Category Management** - Group tasks into custom categories for better organization
- **Beautiful Dark & Light Themes** - Easy on the eyes, day or night
- **Offline First** - All data stored locally on your device for instant access
- **Clean Modern Design** - Minimalist interface with Google Fonts typography

ToDo is designed for people who want a straightforward, reliable task manager without unnecessary complexity. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no ads. Just pure productivity.

Download ToDo today and start accomplishing more with less stress.
